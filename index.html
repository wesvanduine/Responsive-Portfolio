About Me

Born and raised near Seattle, WA. I am a die hard Seattle sports fan, Go Hawks! I moved down to Arizona to attend Arizona State University in 2002. After graduation I decided to stay in Arizona. I loved the weather too much, and the fact I could golf year round. Last year I married my wife and we recently settled into a house in Chandler.

For the past 12 years I have worked for a small company that manufactures and sells elevator lighting and ventilation equipment. I've done almost everything there. From building the actual products, purhcasing raw marterials, to my current position of sales manager.

Over the past few months I have decided to pursue a new venture in computer programming and web development. I am currently attending a coding bootcamp through University of Arizona, which I will complete in September.
